GENRE
  Real-Time Strategy (RTS) — Idle/Management hybrid

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CORE CONCEPT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  A resource-gathering RTS where the player recruits workers to harvest the
  environment, constructs buildings, and trains troops that automatically march
  toward the enemy's castle. The player cannot directly control units — victory
  comes from smart base management, not micromanagement.

  Objective: Destroy the enemy's castle.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RESOURCE GATHERING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Workers are recruited units that autonomously chop trees and mine rocks.
  - Harvesting clears space for building and yields resources.
  - Player starts with 1 worker. Additional workers are recruited using Gold.
  - Workers automatically find and move to the nearest available resource node.

  Resources:
    - Wood       (from trees)
    - Stone      (from rocks)
    - Gold       (earned passively — see Economy)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BUILDINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Constructed using Wood and/or Stone.
  - Can only be placed on cleared land (no trees or rocks).
  - Unlock the ability to recruit troops.
  - Examples (placeholder):
      - Barracks     : Allows recruiting infantry troops
      - Castle/Keep  : Player's base — if destroyed, game over

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TROOPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Recruited by the player from buildings (costs Gold).
  - Each troop recruited increases passive Gold income.
  - After spawning, troops automatically walk toward the enemy's castle
    and attack enemies in their path.
  - Player cannot directly command individual units.
  - Different troop types have different stats (speed, damage, health, cost).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ECONOMY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Gold is earned passively; income rate increases each time a troop is recruited.
  - Gold is spent on:
      - Recruiting additional workers
      - Recruiting troops
      - Upgrades (future feature)
  - Encourages a steady cycle: earn gold → recruit troops → earn more gold.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMBAT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Troops auto-march toward the enemy base and engage enemies automatically.
  - The enemy castle has HP — reduce it to zero to win.
  - The player's castle also has HP — defend it by producing enough troops.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PLAYER ROLE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  The player is a strategic director, not a commander. Their job is:
    1. Direct workers to gather resources (automated after assigning)
    2. Expand and build a productive base
    3. Recruit workers and troops using Gold
    4. Balance worker count vs. troop production vs. upgrades

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
POTENTIAL FEATURES (FUTURE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Enemy AI that mirrors the player's loop (gathers, builds, recruits)
  - Multiple troop tiers with unlock progression
  - Tech tree / upgrades for buildings and units
  - Multiple worker types (faster choppers, miners, etc.)
  - Multiple maps with different terrain layouts
  - Wave-based or continuous enemy pressure modes
